**Installation**

-	Unzip the contents into your UserInterface directory
		e.g. 	C:\Program Files\EA GAMES\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\
		
		The directory paths should look like this after installing the partial skin:
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Legacy-II-Runebook\Interface.xml
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Legacy-II-Runebook\LegacyFiles\Interface.xml
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Legacy-II-Runebook\LegacyFiles\Source\legacy-ui-templates.xml
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Legacy-II-Runebook\LegacyFiles\Textures\legacy-buttons.dds
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Legacy-II-Runebook\LegacyFiles\Textures\legacy-gumps-runebook.dds
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Source\Generic\Runebook.lua
				<where you installed the new client>\Ultima Online Stygian Abyss\UserInterface\Source\Generic\Runebook.xml
				
- Start up the Enhanced (SA) Client and "Legacy II" should appear in the UI selection box at the upper left corner of the login screen, or you can select it from the Interface tab of the User Settings window.
